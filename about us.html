About Arnapurna Neurological Hospital

Leading the way in exceptional neurological care since 1995. We combine cutting-edge medical technology with compassionate care to provide the best possible outcomes for our patients.

Our Story

Arnapurna Neurological Hospital was founded in 1995 with a simple mission: to provide world-class neurological and general healthcare services that are accessible, affordable, and patient-centered. Over the past three decades, we have grown from a small community clinic to a comprehensive neurological center.

Today, we serve over 50,000 patients annually across multiple specialties, with a team of more than 200 healthcare professionals dedicated to your wellbeing.

Our state-of-the-art facilities include advanced diagnostic equipment, modern surgical suites, and comfortable patient rooms designed to support healing and recovery.

Our Mission

To deliver exceptional neurological and general healthcare services with compassion, integrity, and excellence. We strive to improve the health and wellbeing of our community through innovative medical practices and personalized patient care.

Our Vision

To be the leading neurological care provider in the region, recognized for our clinical excellence, patient satisfaction, and commitment to advancing medical science. We envision a healthier future for all through accessible, quality healthcare.
